ABOUT

SINA Hospital is a holding company that was founded in 2020. Starting from the determination to create a Hospital of choice for Indonesian families that provides excellent, ethical, friendly service, provides a sense of security, comfort and protection, as well as a second home for Indonesian families. Currently, the hospital has grown rapidly with the advancement of services and service coverage including the number, facilities, and infrastructure, so it is expected to be able to meet the needs of health services as a qualified type C hospital in its domicile area. In addition, he has a long-term goal to build a Hospital University that educates all human resources needed in hospital operations.
